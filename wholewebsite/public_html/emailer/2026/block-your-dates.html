ABOUT GLOBAL LEADERSHIP SUMMIT VISION 2047
 

Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047 – Delhi (GLS Vision 2047) is a premier platform dedicated to empowering leaders, innovators, thinkers, and changemakers from across industries. Designed as a high-impact forum, the summit will bring together global visionaries, corporate leaders, policymakers, entrepreneurs, educators, and emerging professionals to collaborate, exchange ideas, and shape the future of leadership.

At the heart of GLS Vision 2047 is a commitment to fostering transformational leadership—leadership that inspires innovation, drives sustainable growth, and builds resilient organisations for the future. The summit provides a dynamic environment where participants gain strategic insights, expand their global network, and explore new leadership paradigms that are shaping today’s competitive world.

Hosting the summit in Delhi—India’s hub for business and policy—the event becomes a dynamic gateway for knowledge exchange, leadership excellence, and global collaboration. It brings together world-renowned speakers for inspiring keynote sessions, impactful panel discussions, immersive workshops, hands-on leadership labs, and exclusive networking opportunities that foster meaningful conversations and deliver practical, actionable insights.
