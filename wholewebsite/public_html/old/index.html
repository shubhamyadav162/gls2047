About Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047

Leadership • Innovation • Vision 2047

Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047 convenes India's brightest minds and global capital to forge the nation's Vision 2047. Join the elite gathering where leadership meets investment and innovation.

Hosted at the iconic Yashobhumi in Delhi, this two-day summit brings together the most influential voices in technology, business, policy, and entrepreneurship. As India charts its course toward becoming a developed nation by 2047, Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047 serves as the premier platform for strategic dialogue, partnership formation, and innovation showcase.

GLS Vision 2047 Leaders

Global Body of IIT Alumni Driving National Growth

GLS Vision 2047 represents over 500,000 IIT alumni worldwide, forming one of the most influential networks of engineers, entrepreneurs, and leaders globally.

From founding unicorns to leading Fortune 500 companies, from advising governments to pioneering breakthrough technologies, GLS Vision 2047 alumni have been at the forefront of India's transformation into a global powerhouse.

The organization serves as a catalyst for collaboration, innovation, and nation-building initiatives that leverage the collective expertise and resources of the IIT community.

GLS Vision 2047's Role in Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047

  • Brand custodian ensuring excellence and credibility
  • Global IIT representation and alumni mobilization
