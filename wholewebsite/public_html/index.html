About Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047

Leadership • Innovation • Vision 2047

GLS Vision 2047 Alumni India is an umbrella organization, representing alumni of all Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). GLS Vision 2047 is a not-for-profit Society under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 with Registration No. S-56680 of 2006 dated 20 September 2006 duly registered with the Registrar of Societies, Government of NCT of Delhi, Delhi. It is the Indian legal entity of the global GLS Vision 2047 movement.

The movement, over the years, has developed and and spread in various parts of the world. Besides India, it has a very active presence in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Korea, Singapore, and Australia..

  • GLS Vision 2047 is the global body of IIT alumni, representing 5,00,000+ IITians from India’s most prestigious institutions.
  • A driving force in leadership, entrepreneurship, and national growth.

    THEME

    TECHNOLOGY | INVESTMENTS I GOVERNANCE I DIPLOMACY

    Technology

    IITians have been pivotal contributors in this technological evolution across the world. From founding groundbreaking startups to leading R&D at Fortune 500 companies, IIT alumni consistently push the boundaries of what is possible. Their work in AI, semiconductor design, fintech, climate tech, and space engineering has influenced global standards and accelerated progress in multiple sectors. IITians also play key roles in government, policy-making, and academia, shaping technology frameworks that impact millions.

    Investments

    IIT Alumni are a powerhouse in India’s startup ecosystem. Out of 108 unicorns, 68 have at least one IIT graduate behind them —think Flipkart or Paytm. IIT founders have attracted nearly half of India’s total equity funding in the past decade, driving fintech and deep-tech growth. With strong problem-solving skills and global networks, many are now shifting to the investor side as angels and VCs, pumping billions back into the ecosystem.

    VC trends in India are also heating up. Funding is rebounding sharply—October alone saw $5.3B across 100 deals, up 81% year-on-year. H1 hit $5.7B, led by global players like Sequoia and Tiger.

    Governance

    Strong governance is a growth accelerator. Stable policies, low corruption, and faster bureaucracy have helped India shift from a “stalling” to a “scaling” economy. Reforms like GST, the Insolvency Code, and major digital infrastructure upgrades have improved India’s Ease of Doing Business by 29 ranks since 2016. Better courts and dispute resolution cut economic drag by 3.5% last year, while stronger institutions pulled in $21B in FDI, boosting manufacturing and jobs. Challenges remain—land laws and red tape still slow infrastructure—but good governance consistently turns disorder into economic opportunity.

    Digital infrastructure shows how governance pays off. UPI now processes 12 billion transactions a month, powering small-business growth. Aadhaar’s 920 million verified IDs reduce fraud and speed up welfare delivery.

    Diplomacy

    Diplomacy is powering India’s economic rise. Big deals like the India-EFTA pact and the US iCET partnership are unlocking investments, boosting chip and AI collaborations, and pushing FDI up 18% to $35B. Platforms like the Quad and G20 are strengthening supply chains and easing global market access, while smoother visas help India pull top talent. Geopolitical bumps remain, but overall, foreign policy is turning India into a magnet for capital and innovation.

    Trade agreements amplify the momentum. India now has 13 FTAs and 6 PTAs, driving growth with neighbors like Bhutan, Nepal, and Bangladesh through low- or zero-tariff access.

    GLS Vision 2047 ZONES

    FiDi Financial District Pavilion

    The FiDi Financial District Pavilion serves as the financial backbone of the Global Leadership Summit. It brings together banks, angel investors, venture capital firms, and equity funds to foster investment opportunities and financial collaboration. This pavilion provides a platform for capital deployment, strategic partnerships, and discussions on global financial trends, enabling sustainable economic growth and innovation.

    Entrepreneur Zone

    The Entrepreneur Zone is a dynamic hub for startups, minicorns, and soonicorns to showcase innovation and disruptive ideas. This pavilion encourages entrepreneurial leadership, business scaling, and collaboration with investors and industry experts. It aims to nurture emerging enterprises and empower entrepreneurs to transform ideas into globally competitive ventures.

    State Pavilion

    The State Pavilion highlights the economic, cultural, and investment potential of Indian states such as Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Delhi, and others. It promotes inter-state collaboration, policy awareness, and regional development opportunities. This pavilion serves as a bridge between state governments, investors, and industry leaders.

    Diplomatic Pavilion

    The Diplomatic Pavilion hosts embassies and foreign fund companies, promoting international cooperation and cross-border investment. It facilitates dialogue on global trade, diplomacy, and international partnerships. This pavilion strengthens bilateral and multilateral relationships, encouraging global economic integration and leadership collaboration.

    Ministerial / Governance Zone

    The Ministerial and Governance Zone focuses on public policy, MSME development, and skill enhancement initiatives. It highlights government programs and leadership in governance that support entrepreneurship and workforce development. This pavilion promotes collaboration between policymakers, institutions, and industry to drive inclusive growth.

    Technology / AI Pavilion

    The Technology and AI Pavilion showcases advancements in deep-tech, cloud computing, blockchain, quantum technologies, and artificial intelligence. It highlights the role of technology-driven leadership in shaping the future. Participants explore innovation, digital transformation, and responsible tech solutions for global challenges.

    Energy Pavilion

    The Energy Pavilion focuses on the future of power and sustainability, featuring participants from EVs, solar, wind, oil, and gas sectors. It promotes leadership in energy transition, clean technologies, and energy security. This pavilion supports dialogue on sustainable energy solutions for global development.

    Med Tech Pavilion

    The Med Tech Pavilion showcases innovations in medical technology and assistive technology. It emphasizes leadership in healthcare innovation, accessibility, and patient-centric solutions. This pavilion connects healthcare innovators, policymakers, and investors to advance global health outcomes.

    Food Park Pavilion

    The Food Park Pavilion brings together processed food manufacturers and ingredient providers to highlight advancements in food innovation and supply chains. It focuses on food security, quality, and sustainability. This pavilion promotes leadership in agri-business and food processing for a growing global population.

    Defence Pavilion

    The Defence Pavilion highlights advancements in defense manufacturing, technology, and strategic capabilities. It focuses on national security, innovation, and global defense collaboration. This pavilion fosters dialogue between industry leaders, policymakers, and strategic partners.

    Fintech Impact Pavilion

    The Fintech Impact Pavilion features fintech companies driving financial inclusion and digital transformation. It highlights leadership in digital payments, lending, and financial services innovation. This pavilion explores the impact of fintech solutions on global economies and inclusive growth.

    Supply Chain & Logistics Pavilion

    This pavilion focuses on smart, resilient, and efficient supply chain and logistics solutions. It highlights leadership in transportation, warehousing, and global trade facilitation. Participants explore innovations that strengthen connectivity and optimize global supply networks.

    Smart Manufacturing Pavilion

    The Smart Manufacturing Pavilion showcases advanced manufacturing technologies and Industry 4.0 solutions. It emphasizes automation, robotics, and digital manufacturing leadership. This pavilion supports innovation-driven industrial growth and global competitiveness.

    Consumer Tech Pavilion

    The Consumer Tech Pavilion features companies from retail and FMCG sectors. It highlights leadership in consumer innovation, digital retail, and evolving market trends. This pavilion focuses on enhancing consumer experience and sustainable consumption.

    Infrastructure Pavilion

    The Infrastructure Pavilion brings together real estate developers and road construction companies. It focuses on leadership in urban development, smart cities, and infrastructure growth. This pavilion promotes sustainable and resilient infrastructure for economic advancement.

    Brand Equity Launch Pad

    The Brand Equity Launch Pad is a strategic platform for brand launches, visibility, and market positioning. It enables organizations to strengthen brand identity and connect with global stakeholders. This pavilion highlights leadership in branding, communication, and market influence.

    Global Leaders’ Club

    The Global Leaders’ Club is an exclusive networking pavilion for distinguished leaders, policymakers, investors, and industry captains. It facilitates high-level dialogue, strategic collaboration, and leadership exchange. This space fosters meaningful global partnerships.

    Women Leaders’ Forum

    The Women Leaders’ Forum celebrates and promotes women in leadership across industries. It focuses on empowerment, inclusivity, and gender equality in global leadership. This pavilion encourages dialogue, mentorship, and collaboration for a more balanced leadership ecosystem.

    Global Leaders TV

    Global Leaders TV is a live media and content platform featuring podcasts, interviews, and live streaming. It captures leadership insights, success stories, and summit highlights. This pavilion amplifies global voices and extends the summit’s impact worldwide.
