Refund & Cancellation Policy

Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047

Effective Date: November 8, 2026

Thank you for registering for the Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047 (“GLS Vision 2047”, “we”, “our”, “us”). Please read this Refund & Cancellation Policy carefully, as it outlines our practices regarding cancellations, substitutions, and refunds for paid passes or registrations.

1. General Policy

All registrations and payments made for GLS Vision 2047 through our official website (glsvision2047.com) or authorized partners are considered final. As a large-scale international summit, we incur substantial pre-event costs to plan and deliver the experience; therefore, we follow a strict no-refund policy for confirmed registrations.

2. No Refund Policy

  • Once registration is completed and payment is processed successfully, the registration fee is non-refundable under any circumstances, including inability to attend, visa issues, travel restrictions, or scheduling conflicts.
  • This policy applies to all ticket types and participation categories (e.g., Platinum, Gold, Silver, Startup Pavilion, Hackathon entries, etc.).

3. Substitutions

If a registered participant cannot attend, a substitute delegate from the same organization may be nominated. Requests for substitution must meet the following conditions:

  • The request must be sent in writing to care@glsvision2047.com before January 10, 2026.
  • All substitutions are subject to approval by the organizing committee and may require identity verification.

4. Event Cancellation or Postponement

In the unlikely event that GLS Vision 2047 is postponed, rescheduled, or canceled due to unforeseen circumstances (including but not limited to natural disasters, pandemics, or government directives), the following terms apply:

  • All existing registrations will automatically be transferred to the rescheduled event date.
  • If the participant cannot attend the new dates, a substitute may be nominated as per Section 3.
  • In extreme situations, the organizing committee may consider partial refunds or credit vouchers at its sole discretion.

5. Duplicate Payments

In case of duplicate payments made due to technical error or multiple submissions, please email care@glsvision2047.com within 7 business days. Upon verification, one of the transactions will be refunded to the original source of payment within 10–15 working days.

6. Transaction Security

All online transactions are processed securely through our payment partner PayU. We do not store credit/debit card or banking information on our servers. Payment confirmations are sent via email upon successful processing.

For payment-related queries or refund requests under the limited exceptions mentioned above, please contact our finance and registration desk:
