Welcome to the official website of the Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047 (“GLS Vision 2047”, “we”, “our”, “us”). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy in accordance with applicable data protection and privacy laws, including the Information Technology Act (India), GDPR (EU), and other relevant global standards.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and safeguard personal information provided by users who visit our website at https://glsvision2047.com, register for events, submit forms, or engage with our online platforms. By using our website, you consent to the practices outlined below.

Your information may be used for the following legitimate purposes:

  • To register you for the Global Leadership Summit Vision 2047 and related programs.
  • To issue payment confirmations, invoices, and participation details.
  • To send you important updates, announcements, and logistical details regarding the event.
  • To respond to inquiries, feedback, and partnership requests.
  • To enhance our website performance, user experience, and service offerings.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our terms of service.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (e.g., registration, ticket purchase).

  • Legitimate interest in improving our services and communications.

We do not sell or trade your personal information. However, we may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers strictly for the purposes mentioned above, including:

All such partners are bound by strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.

Our website uses cookies and analytics tools to enhance usability and track visitor engagement. You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit certain functionalities of our website.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for event operations, compliance, and legitimate business purposes. Once data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Data Security

We implement advanced security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure, including SSL encryption, restricted data access, and secure server hosting. However, no system is entirely immune to cyber risks, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

As a global event, your data may be processed or stored on servers located outside your home country. We ensure that any such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and are subject to adequate safeguards.
