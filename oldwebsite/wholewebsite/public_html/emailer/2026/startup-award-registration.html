ABOUT GEDC 2025
 

The Global Energy Digitalisation Conclave (GEDC 2025 START-UP AWARDS) have been created to recognise the entrepreneur, innovators and technology in the Energy Digitalisation Sector. The objective of the GEDC 2025 START-UP AWARDS is to celebrate the industry’s most outstanding achievements.

 
NOMINATION BENEFITS
  • Opportunity to win the awards and get national recognition
  • Brand Visibility, Valuable PR and Business connects
  • Connect With thought leaders, mentors, investors and industry leaders
  • Business Development Opportunities
 
CATEGORY FOR AWARDS
  • Startup leadership award in energy digitisation
  • Best Digital innovation award in energy generation and distribution
  • Best service provider award in energy sector
  • Entrepreneur of the year (Innovation in Technology in energy business)
  • Energy transition award (Start-Up and young companies who are working on ideas affecting global energy transition and climate change in line with international commitments)
