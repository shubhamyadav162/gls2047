ABOUT GEDC 2025
 

Welcome to the 3rd Edition of the Global Energy Digitalisation Conclave (GEDC 2025), where industry leaders, innovators, and policymakers converge to shape the future of energy through digital transformation. This premier event is designed to empower energy sector stakeholders with the latest advancements in digital technologies, enhancing efficiency, resilience, and sustainability across the industry.

As we enter a new era of energy, digitalisation is at the core of optimizing resources, reducing emissions, and meeting the growing global demand for cleaner, smarter energy solutions. The Global Energy Digitalisation Conclave (GEDC 2025) brings together experts from across the globe to explore cutting-edge technologies like IoT, AI, big data, and blockchain and their applications in modernizing energy systems.

Join us at GEDC 2025 to connect with visionaries, explore transformative solutions, and drive the energy sector towards a greener, digital future. Together, let’s lead the charge in building a sustainable and digitally empowered energy ecosystem.
